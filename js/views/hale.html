Richard M. Hale Cap & Skull Scholarship

On an annual basis, the Cap & Skull Society awards the Richard M. Hale Cap & Skull Memorial Scholarship to rising juniors exhibiting the qualities of academic excellence and extracurricular involvement early in their college careers, regardless of financial need.

To be eligible for the Scholarship, students must:
  1. achieve a minimum 3.0 cumulative grade point average after the first term of their sophomore year
  2. be enrolled in one of the following undergraduate schools at Rutgers - New Brunswick:
    • School of Arts and Sciences
    • Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy
    • School of Engineering
    • Mason Gross School of the Arts

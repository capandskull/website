Richard M. Hale Cap & Skull Scholarship

On an annual basis, the Cap & Skull Society awards The Richard M. Hale Cap & Skull Memorial Scholarship to sophomores exhibiting the qualities of academic excellence and extracurricular involvement early in their college careers regardless of financial need. To be eligible for the Scholarship, students must achieve a minimum 3.0 cumulative grade point average after the first term of their sophomore year and be enrolled in any undergraduate school at Rutgers, New Brunswick/Piscataway (including the School of Arts and Sciences, the School of Environmental and Biological Sciences, the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy, the School of Engineering, the Rutgers Business School, the College of Nursing, the Bloustein School, the School of Communication and Information, the School of Management and Labor Relations, the School of Social Work, or the Mason Gross School of the Arts).

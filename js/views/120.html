Founder's Day

Overview

Join us on Saturday, January 18th at 6pm at the Rutgers Club for an evening of celebration as we kick off our 120th anniversary! Enjoy food and cocktails as you catch up with your classmates while hearing about everything we have in store for 2020 and beyond. Questions? Email events@capandskull.org.

120th Anniversary Gala Weekend

Celebrate our 120th anniversary all weekend long! Join us on the weekend of November 7-8 for Convocation in Kirkpatrick Chapel when we’ll pin the class of 2020, lunch at the Alumni House, and afternoon activities featuring alumni of all decades before the main event - our evening decade dinner gala. We’ll cap the weekend off with a jazz brunch on Sunday. Stay tuned for a complete schedule and registration details. Questions? Email events@capandskull.org.
